Report on a visit to one local restaurant

My girlfriend and I visited a little Thailand restaurant in Berlin, Friedrichshain last week. It is very small but comfortable. Most China and Thai restaurants are kitsch, but here the decoration is plain and simple. Behind the bar you can see how the food is made.

This is not a restaurant for a long dinner evening. If you order a meal you will get it in few minutes. But the meal isn't only a snack. It tastes delicious and the portions are not small.

Important not for English tongues: Thai cuisine use a lot of spices! If a dish on the menu says "spicy" then it is "hot" (for English tongues)! Maybe you think ginger is not a hot spice. Order a Thai meal with ginger and think about it. Don't forget to wipe your sweat away from your forehead.
