Reported questions

a Wh questions:
What time is it, Jenny? -- Nick asked Jenny what time it was.
Do not forget to change the word order and omit the question mark, because in reported speech these are not question any more.

b Yes/No questions:
"Are you coming to the cinema, Jenny?" -- Nick asked Jenny whether/if she was coming to the cinema.

c Requests:
"Would you pass the salt please, Sid?"
This type of question can be reported in full:
Tom asked Sid if he would pass him the salt.
or with an infinitive, which summaries the action:
Tom asked Sid to pass him the salt.

d Orders: usually an imperative:
"Go to your room!" -- Sarah's mother told her to go to her room.
