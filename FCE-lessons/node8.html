Present simple (I do)

Use the simple for things in general or things that happen repeatedly. In many languages, the present simple is used to describe what is happening now. Notice that this is not the case in English.
- to talk about things which are always true:
Water boils at 100^ centigrade.
He has blue eyes.
- to talk about habits and routines:
He always goes to bed before midnight.
What do you do? (What is your job?)
