Time and money expressions as adjectives

When the price or the time are used as adjectives, they have no plural form.
Adjective + noun
It's a four-hour flight from Birmingham to Athens. (It takes four hours.)
Adjective+noun
He had to pay a ten-pound parking fine. (He had to pay ten pounds.)
