Future time

will + infinitive -- decisions, offers, promises
This form is used when we make decisions, offers and promises at the time of speaking:
What shall I have? Let's see ... Oh yes, I'll have a salad. (I'm deciding now.)
A: Have a lovely holiday!
B: Thanks. I'll send you a card. (I promise.)
A: Oh no, I've left my bag in the car.
B: I'll go back and get it for you. (I offer.)
