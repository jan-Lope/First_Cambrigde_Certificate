Used to + infinitive

This tells us about things that happened in the past but which don't happen now. These are things which happened regulary over a long period of time.
He used to play football when he was a child.
Does he play football now? (no) -- Did he play football often (yes)
Used to does not exist in the present. The present equivalent of used to is usually.
