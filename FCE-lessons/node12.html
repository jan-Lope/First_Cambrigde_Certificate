Auxiliary Verbs -- Modal Verbs

This is illogical in English:
You need a modal verb for a future form (will), but you need auxiliary verbs for other time forms (be, have).

Auxiliary Verbs
have (has, had)   haben (Present/Past perfect)
do (did, does)   tun, machen (in Fragen, Negationen)
to be (was, were, been, beeing, is)   sein (... continuous)


Modal Verbs
can   können
could   kann, könnte
may   kann; mag; darf
might   kann, könnte, mag
will   werden (future)
would   wollte; würde
must   müssen
would   wollte; würde
shall   beabsichtigt; sollen
should   sollte; sollten
ought   sollte
