After/After that

After is generally followed by a noun, gerund or phrase:
After coffee we went home.
After drinking our coffee we went home.
After we had drunk our coffee we went home.
When after is used at the beginning of a sentence, we are saying that the events happened in the order which they are mentioned.
After + first event + second event.
