So/Nor

So is used with modal and auxiliary verbs to agree with positive statements:
I like pizza. -- So do I.
I can swim. -- So can she.

Nor is used to agree with negative statements.
I don't like cabbage. -- Nor do I.
I haven't done my homework. -- Nor has Marion.

So/Nor cannot be used to disagree with statements:
I'm not going to the party. -- Really? I am.
