Prefer/Rather

a I prefer is used to refer to a general situation:
I prefer eating Greek food to French food. (always/usually)
b I'd prefer is used to refer to a specific situation:
I'd prefer to go to a restaurant. (now/on this occasion)
I 'd rather + infinitive without "to" can be used in place of I'd prefer.
