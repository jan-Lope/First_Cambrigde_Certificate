Past simple + Past continuous

The past simple and continuous are used together:
- when one action is interrupted by another:
I was watching TV when the lights went out.
- when one action takes place during a longer one:
When/While I was shopping in Oxford Street I saw a car accident.
I met Tom and Ann at the airport a few weeks ago. They were going to Berlin and I was going to Madrid.
