Looks/Seem/Appears

These phrases are useful for describing photographs:
It looks as if they + verb.
They look + adjective.
It looks like + (a) noun.
She seems to be + adjective.
She seems to be + verb + -ing.
He appears to be + adjective.
He appears to be + verb + -ing.

I get the impression that he is ...
Maybe they're ...
Perhaps they're ...
