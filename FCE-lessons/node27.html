Present perfect vs Past simple

If you need to decide which tense to use, ask yourself the question When?
I've never been to Italy, but I went to Greece last summer.
I've never been to Italy, ... When? In my life (Present perfect).
... but I went to Greece last summer. When? Last summer (Past simple).

The Past simple is used to talk about things which happened in periods which have finished.
