Time Indicators

Main changes:

today -- that day
now -- then
this -- that
here -- there
ago -- before/previously
tomorrow -- the next day / the following day
yesterday -- the day before / the previous day
last night -- the night before / the previous night
next week -- the following week / the week after (that)
