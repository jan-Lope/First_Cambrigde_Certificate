Superlatives

The superlative is formed by adding est to one-syllable adjectives, iest to two-syllable adjectives ending in y and most before longer adjectives:

It's the oldest building in the city.
It was the prettiest village I had ever visited.
He is the most enthusiastic student in the class.

Exceptions:
good - (better) / best
bad - (worse) / worst
far - (further) / farther:
